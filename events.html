Rooted in Saint John. Running, Playing, and Competing Together.

We sponsor, organize, and show up for the events that keep Saint John moving.

From the Harbour Splash fun run to local hockey tournaments and youth soccer leagues, Sports & Stuff Saint John is proud to be on the ground supporting our athletic community year-round. We believe sport is one of the best things about life in Saint John, and we're committed to making it more accessible for everyone.
